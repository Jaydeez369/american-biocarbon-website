5X ABSORBENT PELLETS
100% PLANT BASED AND BIODEGRADABLE

American BioCarbon's renewable absorbent pellets absorb up to 5x their own weight
in water, double the capacity of wood-based pellets and roughly five times that of
clay. Manufactured from single-source recycled sugarcane bagasse, they ship and
store stable, then disintegrate on contact to expose the full internal pore
structure.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PLANT-BASED ADVANTAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Made from single-source recycled sugarcane bagasse. The cane stalk carries a native
vascular structure with open parenchyma cells. Natural lignin binds the pellet during
pressing, with no synthetic adhesives in the product or the finished waste.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HONEYCOMB MICROSTRUCTURE HOLDS MORE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Peer-reviewed studies of bagasse-derived sorbents describe a honeycomb-like internal
structure with cavities and channels at the micro and macro scale (Castro et al.,
2017). Native wood porosity is dominated by sub-2-nanometer pathways and slow
capillary uptake (Plötze et al., 2020). Bagasse moves liquid faster, holds more of
it, and breaks down on schedule.
