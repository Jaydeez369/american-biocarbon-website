Rules of the system

Read before adding new UI.

1 · Brand ≠ status

Navy & crimson are identity. Green/amber/red/blue carry meaning. Never use green as a brand accent or navy as "success."

2 · Go through tokens

No raw hex in components. Use a semantic alias (--brand, --success…). Primitives live in tokens.css only.

3 · Crimson is rare

One crimson CTA per view, max. If everything is urgent, nothing is.

4 · One icon language

SVG only, single stroke width, 24px grid, currentColor. No emoji as structural icons.

5 · 4pt rhythm

Spacing in 4/8px steps. Radius from the token ramp. Tabular figures for all data columns.

6 · Contrast holds

Body text ≥ 4.5:1, large/secondary ≥ 3:1 — in both themes, checked independently.
